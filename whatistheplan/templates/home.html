What Is the PLAN?

PLAN (Parkview LAN Party) is a semiannual LAN Party Hosted by the Computer Club of Western Michigan University at the Parkview Engineering Campus.

Attendance is usually 150-200 people, and we are growing every year.

PLAN 26 is Saturday and Sunday April 1st and 2nd, 2017! Click here to sign up now!

Admission is $10 for the whole weekend! ($5 for Computer Club members)

Please direct any questions to plan@yakko.cs.wmich.edu.
