Welcome to Whatistheplan.com!

PLAN (Parkview LAN Party) is a semiannual LAN Party Hosted by the Computer Club of Western Michigan University at the Parkview Engineering Campus.
Attendance is usually 150-200 people, and we are growing every year.


Join us November 12th for PLAN 25!


Admission is $10 for the whole weekend!
